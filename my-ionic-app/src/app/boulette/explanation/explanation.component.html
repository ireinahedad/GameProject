How to play

Each player chooses words without the others knowing. The team that makes its members guess the most words wins.

The game has 3 rounds:

In the first round, the player can use any word (but only words) to make the others guess the word (except the same words or very similar).

In the second round, the player can only use gestures to make the others guess the word.

In the third round, the player can only use one word to make the others guess the word.

Time is limited and depends on the number of words in the game.
